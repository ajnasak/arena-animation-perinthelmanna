Enter a revolutionary world

Today, there's no business that doesn't have an online presence. Digital is slowly yet steadily becoming mainstream and seems to be the need of the hour. That's how UI/UX has become a fundamental part of the digital world; be it through e-commerce, company websites or mobile apps. While UX is focused on the user's journey to solve a problem, UI is focused on how a product's surfaces look and function. These days, there's a growing tendency for companies to hire for very specific roles, such as UX researcher or interaction designer, to cover all of the different aspects of user experience
